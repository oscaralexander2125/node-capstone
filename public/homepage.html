Welcome to Health Tracker

This app is user-friendly. To add your daily routine, just click on add daily routine. Click on Analyze Progress to take a look at your most recent progress or make some corrections. Everything is already in pounds and Kcal so just add the value and don't worry about units. For the meals, those were made loosely to allow the user to just write down their meals in which ever way preferred. Just note that there is only a maximum of 7 meals to write down since most people don't eat more than that in a day.
